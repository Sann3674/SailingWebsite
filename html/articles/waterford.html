Waterford

30th April 2022

A very pleasant town with some extremely friendly folk.



Highlights

Guinness, especially in Henry Downes, awonderful old pub and Jordan’s Bar, a lovely warm welcome.

Cabbage and bacon,great comfort food and a new dish for me.

History In 1170 in Waterford, Princess Aoife married the Norman Lord Strongbow, changing the course of Irish history and introducing Norman power and influence into Ireland.

Moment of levity Christ Church Cathedral WiFi password - ‘blessyou’!

Curraghmore House Home of Marquess of Waterford (visited by Nigel)



Lowlights

Laundry facilities Spin dryer at marina inoperable requiring Little Scarlet to become a drying room with a little help from Ivan the Heater!
